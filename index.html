Welcome to Greatest
Birthday Party 24

Get ready for an unforgettable 18th bash! Join me for a weekend full of fun (April 26-28, 2024), with two nigths hotel in Narty. We're coordinating rides, and the theme is Euphoria/Bond 007 - expect a night of luxury and mystery. Enjoy games like drunken chess and beer pong, and hit the dance floor with everything from folk dances to the Macarena. Don’t forget the take memorable photos and videos with our photographer. Be a part of this spectacular night!

Willa in Narty, Warmian-Masurian, Poland

Meet at Garleria Warmińska

By Bartosz Event-Main-Brain
Zaczynamy nasze urodzinowe przygody od spotkania przy wejściu do największego centrum handlowego w regionie, Galerii Warmińskiej. Auchan Adventure: Shopping and Gathering Supplies

By Bartosz Event-Main-Brain
We’ll split into smaller teams, each responsible for different shopping categories: food, beverages, decorations, and gifts. This isn't just about efficiency; it's also about enjoyment and friendly competition. Let's see which team can manage their funds best and complete their shopping list first!

Arrival at airbnb and beginning of party

By Bartosz Event-Main-Brain
As we enter the Airbnb, the atmosphere is instantly inviting, with ambient lighting and a playlist of the birthday person’s favorite songs playing in the background. Each guest is greeted with a welcome drink, setting the tone for a night of warmth and merriment. Unpacking Supplies: We'll unpack our bounty from Auchan. The kitchen becomes a hive of activity as the barbecue ingredients are prepped and the party snacks are artistically arranged on platters. The refrigerators are stocked with refreshments, and the bubbly is put on ice.
